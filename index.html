About company
Crystal Water Technologies is a distributor and technology partner for solutions in the field of environmental water purification systems, industrial and heavy equipment, trade, and spare parts.

We help our customers to contribute to the preservation of the planet resources by finding solutions from recycled materials, develop eco-friendly production process and mining technologies.

Water purification equipment
We pay special attention to water purification system technologies, offering clients a wide range of reliable equipment – from innovative filtration systems that remove impurities to advanced disinfection methods.

To minimize the negative impact of anthropogenic factors on nature and human health, we offer effective water purification solutions for residential, commercial, and industrial use: mechanical, chemical, and biological purification devices, submersible and recirculation equipment.

At the initial stage of each project, we always conduct water quality analysis and deep environment studies. The selection and installation of water purification systems at Crystal Water Technologies are carried out in accordance with the ISO 14001:2015 "Environmental Management Systems" and ISO 9001:2015 "Quality Management Systems" standards.
